Financial Basics

Needs vs. Wants

A Need is something you must have to survive (food, shelter). A Want is something that makes life fun but isn't essential (video games, designer shoes).

The 50/30/20 Rule

Try putting 50% toward needs, 30% toward wants, and 20% toward savings!
